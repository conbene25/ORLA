# ORLA - Open Resource & Learning Assistant
ORLA consists of two parts:
* An Open Access Self-Assessment Tool that provides you with an evaluation of where you stand on your journey of making your cultural collections available with open access, plus indicating what might be suitable first/next steps for you to take.
* An Open Resourece & Learning Assistant that can point you to useful resources, tools, experts, services that might help you take these first/next steps.
